How Video Consultations Work

Important Notice

Video consultations are not a replacement for emergency medical services. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please call our helpline at 080 2572 2573 or visit your nearest emergency room.

Tips for a Successful Consultation
  • Find a quiet, private space for your call
  • Test your camera and microphone before starting
  • Have a list of symptoms or questions ready
  • Keep a pen and paper nearby to take notes
  • Ensure your device is charged or plugged in
