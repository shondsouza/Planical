Crisis Text Line

Frequently Asked Questions

Planical is a comprehensive mental health platform designed to help you manage stress, anxiety, and improve your overall mental wellbeing. Our platform offers various tools and resources including:

  • Stress detection and analysis
  • Relaxation music and meditation guides
  • Interactive games for cognitive stimulation
  • AI-powered chat support for mental health concerns
  • Educational resources and articles on mental wellness

By using these tools regularly, you can develop better mental health habits and find support when you need it.

Creating an account with Planical is simple:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page
  2. Fill in your name, age, email address, and choose a password
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  4. Log in with your new credentials
  5. Explore the various features and start your mental wellness journey

Once you're logged in, we recommend starting with the stress assessment to get personalized recommendations based on your current mental state.

Our stress detection feature uses a scientifically validated questionnaire to assess your current stress levels. Here's how it works:

  1. Navigate to the Stress Detection section
  2. Answer a series of questions about your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors
  3. Our algorithm analyzes your responses
  4. You receive an assessment indicating whether you're experiencing normal or high stress levels
  5. Based on your results, we provide personalized recommendations for managing your stress

The assessment is not a diagnostic tool, but it can help you understand your stress levels and guide you toward appropriate resources.

We take your privacy and data security very seriously at Planical:

  • All personal data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We use Firebase Authentication to ensure secure login processes
  • Your assessment results and usage data are only accessible to you
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • All communications with our servers are encrypted using HTTPS

For more details about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Planical AI is our conversational assistant designed to provide mental health support and information:

  • Access Planical AI by clicking on the "Planical AI" link in the navigation menu
  • Type your questions or concerns in the chat interface
  • Receive compassionate, informative responses based on mental health best practices
  • Ask about coping strategies, mental health concepts, or get guidance on using Planical features

While Planical AI is a helpful resource, it's important to remember that it's not a replacement for professional mental health care. For serious concerns, please consult with a qualified healthcare provider.

If you're experiencing technical issues with Planical, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh your browser page
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. Log out and log back in to your account

If you're still experiencing issues after trying these steps, please contact our support team through the Contact Support form with details about the problem you're encountering, including any error messages, the device you're using, and the browser version.
